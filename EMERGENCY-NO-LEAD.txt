LEARNO GPC — HUB DIED, EVENT LEAD NOT HERE
============================================

ON THE MAC (hub computer)
-------------------------
1. Double-click:  Start Mac Hub   (on the Desktop)
2. A black window opens — LEAVE IT OPEN
3. Wait ~30 seconds — Master screen opens in the browser
4. If nothing opens, click this link in Safari/Chrome:

   http://localhost:3001/presenter?compact=1

5. On the Master screen, tap the green button:

   Refresh master

ON EACH KIOSK (Windows)
-----------------------
1. Tap the settings cog (staff button on screen)
2. Tap:  Refresh screen

Still offline on Master?
------------------------
- Mac must stay on power and Wi-Fi
- Run set-hub-ip.bat on kiosks with the Mac’s current Wi-Fi IP
  (on Mac Terminal: ipconfig getifaddr en0)

Do NOT close the black “Start Mac Hub” window on the Mac.
